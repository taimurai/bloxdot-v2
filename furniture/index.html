01 · Why local furniture fails

most furniture in Pakistan fails in three very specific ways.

Not a hypothetical. This is the corner of a sofa we replaced for a client in DHA last year · bought new in 2022, done by 2024. Every homeowner we've worked with has lived through at least one of these. We have too.

foam

the foam gives up in 18 months.

What you bought isn't what you're sitting on anymore. The shape is gone. The shop won't re-foam it, and a new sofa costs roughly what the old one did.

frame

the frame cracks at the joint.

Wet wood, stapled joinery, no corner blocks. It holds for a year · long enough to be past the return window. Then something inside starts to creak every time you sit down.

service

the fabric tears before year two.

And when you call the shop, they don't remember you. Furniture here is sold once and never serviced. That's the whole game.

The warranty

5 years on the frame. if it breaks, we fix it or replace it.

One warranty card, numbered and registered to you at delivery. If the frame fails from a manufacturing defect inside five years, we repair or replace the piece at our cost. No wear-and-tear arguments. No small print. Transferable if you sell the piece.

06 · FAQ

things people ask us before they buy.

are bloxdot pieces made in your own workshop?
No. BloxDot designs every piece in-house. Fabrication is handled by trusted partners in Lahore who we have worked with on multiple projects. Every piece passes through our design team for spec, finish, and quality before it ships to a client.
how does pricing work?
Every piece is built to your dimensions, fabric, and finish, so there's no standard price list. At the consultation we go through scope and give you a written estimate before any work begins. Most clients find that custom sits below imported equivalents and above off-the-shelf local furniture.
what's the typical lead time?
4 to 8 weeks from signed spec and deposit, depending on fabric availability and the complexity of the piece. Full-room projects run 8 to 12 weeks. We confirm the exact window in writing at order time and update you weekly during the build.
can i order just one piece, or do i need to do a whole room?
Either works. Plenty of clients start with a dining table or a sofa. Plenty start with a full living room or bedroom suite. The minimum order is one piece.
do you work from an interior designer's brief?
Yes. If you or your designer already has a spec, send it over. We can also bring our own architectural team in if you want help developing the look. No pressure to use both.
can you match pieces i already own?
Usually, yes. Bring the piece itself to consultation, or send photos and fabric samples. We'll match wood tone, finish, and upholstery as closely as the material allows.
how does the warranty actually work?
At delivery we register the piece in your name and hand you a numbered warranty card. Within 5 years, if the frame fails from a manufacturing defect, we repair or replace at our cost. 1-year coverage on upholstery and foam. Claims go straight to us, not a call center.
what happens if i don't like the piece when it arrives?
You shouldn't be surprised at handover. We send fabric samples, finish samples, and build-stage photos during production so you see the piece coming together. If something is wrong at delivery (wrong fabric, dimensions off, finish mismatched), we take it back and make it right at our cost.
